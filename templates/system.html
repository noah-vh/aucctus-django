Agent Architecture

When a user describes a product space, the orchestrator coordinates three specialist agents in parallel. Each agent produces structured data that feeds into a final synthesis verdict.

Input

"Describe a product space to research"

Orchestrator

Coordinates all specialist agents, synthesizes findings, and produces the final verdict with confidence scoring.

Verdict Confidence Risk/Opportunity

Incumbents Agent

Finds competing companies, extracts features, pricing models, market positioning, and identifies whitespace opportunities.

Companies Features Pricing Gaps

Funding Agent

Tracks funding rounds, investor patterns, capital flow trends. Classifies space maturity based on investment patterns.

Rounds Investors Amounts Trends

Growth Agent

Triangulates market size from TAM reports, hiring trends, search interest, and organic demand signals.

TAM CAGR Hiring Search Volume

Features

The platform surfaces research through multiple views, each designed for a different part of the workflow.

Chat

Natural language research interface. Describe a product space and watch agents work in real-time with streaming responses. Verdicts link directly to structured data.

Streaming agents Session history Verdict delivery

Ideas

Portfolio view of all tracked product spaces. See verdict status, confidence trends, and quickly scan which ideas changed since last research.

Score sparklines Status management

Idea Detail

Full drill-down into a single idea. View competitors, funding events, market signals, intelligence alerts, verdict history, and research runs.

6 data sections Expandable lists

Dashboard

Quick research entry point with tracked ideas overview, real-time signal feed, and active job monitoring. The daily driver view.
